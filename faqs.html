It is considered a area as there are many housing properties and business entities that provide various amenities and facilities to cater to the population residing here, as well as direct access to major highways that connect it to other established townships. Pandan Jaya is located to the south-east of downtown Kuala Lumpur. It is adjacent to Ampang to the north and Kajang to the south, both which are major town within the metropolitan area of Greater Kuala Lumpur.
