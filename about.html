    • ABOUT PANDAN JAYA


    Pandan Jaya is township located in Ampang district of Selangor, Malaysia. It is part of the larger Pandan parliamentary constituency. The area is known for its mixed development, comprising residential, commercial, and industrial properties. Residentially, Pandan Jaya consists of various housing options, including apartments, condominiums, and landed properties. The township is characterized by a blend of both old and new developments, offering a diverse range of housing choice to its residents.


    Commercially, Pandan Jaya features a bustling business district with a variety of shops, restaurants, as well as local businesses. The area is known for its vibrant street food scene,with numerous strategies offering a wide range of local cuisines. Most importantly, the transportation in Pandan Jaya is facilitated by major roads and highways, providing easy access to neighbouring areas and the Kuala Lumpur city center. The Pandan Jaya LRT (Light Trail Transit) station serves as a convenient public transportation option for residents and visitors.
