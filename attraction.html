      ★ Jia Li Mian Noodle House

    • This noodle house is famous for its noodle dish. It is non-halal but is greatly recommended for others to eat enjoy! The food is affordable and have variety of foods to choose.

      ★ Restoran Rasik

    • This mamak restaurant is perfect for people that enjoy mamak delicacies. The price is affordable, the place is clean and it is open for 24 hours.

      ★ Pandan Jaya Night Market

    • This night market is open every Tuesday and Saturday night. Many types of stalls, foods and even fresh groceries such as vegetables and seafoods are also being sold here.
      Address: Jalan Pandan 3/2, Pandan Jaya, 55100 Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia
