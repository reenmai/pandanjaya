    Facilities of Pandan Jaya

    LRT Pandan Jaya

    Pandan Jaya Light Rail Transit (LRT) station is located in the Ampang Jaya area of Selangor in Malaysia. It is part of Ampang Line, formerly known as the STAR, Ampang Line, and Sri Petaling Line. It was the first station to open in the STAR system, along with 13 other stations on the Sultan Ismail Ampang line. The station is located close to Pandan Jaya and its name is often confused with Pandan Indah Station, which is 1 km northeast of the station.

    Community Library (PPAS Pandan Jaya)

    PPAS (pronounced Perbadanan perpustakan awam Selangor) is one branch of PPAS. This branch is located at Pandan Jaya, Malaysia. It is sometimes called a community library.

    What is a community library?

    A community library is a public library that is not a part of an area's statutory library provision. It is not operated or fully funded by the local or national government. A community library offers library services to the people of the local or regional community. A community library may be owned and operated by community groups, charitable organisations, non-governmental organizations and other organisations. However, it may also receive some public funds from the local authority based on various funding models.
